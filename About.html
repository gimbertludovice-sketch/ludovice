Who We Are

A kitchen that started on a two-burner stove

LUDOVICE began in 2025 as a weekend delivery service run out of a Biñan condo kitchen. Chef Gimbert Ludovice was cooking the dishes his grandmother taught him — sinigang, adobo, kare-kare — for coworkers who missed home-cooked Filipino food during long work weeks.

Word spread. Within a year, the two-burner stove became a full commercial kitchen, and the weekend side hustle became LUDOVICE Kitchen & Table: a Filipino-fusion menu of 600 dishes, six categories, one obsession — cooking food that tastes like it was made for you specifically.

Milestones

Company Timeline

From a condo kitchen to 600 dishes — a lot of rice along the way.

2025

Weekend delivery, condo kitchen

Chef Gimbert starts taking pre-orders on Facebook for 12 dishes, cooked Friday through Sunday.

2025

First commercial kitchen in Biñan

Demand triples. LUDOVICE leases its first commissary kitchen and hires four cooks.

2025

Menu expands to 6 categories

Appetizers, mains, sides, drinks, desserts, and a rotating Chef's Specials line launch together.

2026

600-dish catalog, citywide delivery

LUDOVICE crosses 600 menu items and expands delivery coverage across Biñan Laguna.

2026

LUDOVICE goes fully online

This storefront launches — full ordering, accounts, and checkout, built around the same 600-item menu.
